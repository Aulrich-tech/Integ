Reservations

You can reserve a table by calling us directly or sending a message through our Contacts page. Please include your preferred date, time, and number of guests so we can prepare accordingly.

Yes! We just ask that you let us know at least 24 hours before your scheduled reservation so we can open the slot for other guests. Last-minute cancellations may affect future booking priority.

Group reservations are welcome for parties of 8 or more. For smaller groups, walk-ins are generally accommodated based on availability. Contact us in advance for a smoother experience.
Payments

We accept cash, GCash, Maya, and major credit/debit cards. For online orders, payment is processed through our secure checkout. Cash on delivery is available for select areas only.

Please screenshot your payment confirmation and send it to us immediately via our Contacts page or messenger. Our team will verify and either confirm your order or process a full refund within 24 hours.
Tip: Always save your transaction reference number — it makes verification much faster.

Yes. Refunds are offered for wrong or incomplete orders, failed transactions, and event cancellations made at least 48 hours in advance. Processed within 3–5 business days depending on your payment method.
Events & Catering

Yes! We cater for birthdays, corporate lunches, family reunions, and other occasions. Our packages include food, setup, and serving staff. Contact us at least 5 days in advance to discuss your needs.

Absolutely! Private bookings are available for full or partial restaurant use. Reach out through our Contacts page with your preferred date, time, and party size so we can send you our event packages.

We recommend at least 5–7 days ahead for small events and 2–3 weeks for larger gatherings. This ensures we can source fresh ingredients and prepare everything to our usual standard.
