OUR HISTORY

'House of Us' Restaurant is a small restaurant first established in 2019, located at 7 Tamaraw Hills, Marulas, Valenzuela City. Our prices are very affordable so we can cater to different statuses of customers, yet all foods are made fresh upon ordering, so we can say that we are not a fast-food restaurant.

In 2024, we decided to expand the restaurant and opened our second location at 102 Caimito Road, Brgy. 80, Caloocan City. With the help of God, it is operating well, and we aim to expand outside Metro Manila with the help of your establishment.

House of Us Restaurant offers different dishes around the world such as SILOG MEALS (rice meals), which are dominant in the Philippines, sushi rolls and baked sushi from Japan, fresh salad rolls (Vietnamese style), bibimbap (Korean), pasta and burgers (American), and drinks such as fruit teas and milk teas, and many more.

NATURE OF OPERATION

House of Us Restaurant operates as a casual dining establishment that offers a variety of freshly prepared local and international dishes at affordable prices. We focus on quality food, diverse menu offerings, and customer satisfaction, ensuring that every meal is prepared upon order and served with care.

Culinary Services

We offer a wide selection of freshly prepared dishes including Filipino silog meals, Japanese sushi and baked sushi, Vietnamese fresh rolls, Korean bibimbap, and American-style pasta and burgers. Each dish is carefully prepared using quality ingredients to ensure great taste and customer satisfaction.

Beverage Services

We provide refreshing drinks such as fruit teas, milk teas, and other beverages that complement the meals and enhance the dining experience. Our beverages are made with quality ingredients to provide refreshing flavors that perfectly match our dishes.

Customer Service & Dining

We maintain a clean, comfortable, and welcoming environment while delivering attentive service to ensure customer satisfaction and a pleasant dining experience. Our staff is dedicated to providing friendly and efficient service to make every visit enjoyable and memorable.

OUR MISSION

We are committed to maintaining consistency in quality, enhancing our service standards, and creating a warm and welcoming environment where every customer feels valued, satisfied, and eager to return.

Our Milestones

The growth of House of Us Restaurant reflects our strong and continuous commitment to delivering quality food, maintaining affordability, and ensuring exceptional customer satisfaction. From its humble beginnings, the restaurant has steadily developed through consistent effort, dedication, and improvement in both products and services. Each milestone achieved along the way represents not only a significant step in our business expansion but also the challenges we have overcome and the lessons we have learned as we continue to improve our operations. These milestones serve as a clear reflection of our long-term vision and determination to grow sustainably while staying true to our core values of providing fresh, delicious meals and a welcoming dining experience for every customer.

Year

2019

Establishment of the Restaurant

We officially established House of Us Restaurant at 7 Tamaraw Hills, Marulas, Valenzuela City. Starting as a small family-run dining business, we focused on offering affordable yet high-quality meals that are freshly prepared upon order. Our goal was to serve customers from different walks of life while maintaining a warm and welcoming dining environment that emphasizes freshness and value.

Year

2021

Menu Expansion

We expanded our menu to include a wider variety of international dishes such as Japanese sushi, Korean bibimbap, Vietnamese fresh rolls, and American-style pasta and burgers. This expansion allowed us to cater to more diverse tastes and preferences while maintaining our commitment to freshly prepared meals. It also helped position our restaurant as a place for both local and international cuisine.

Year

2023

Strengthening Community Presence

We continued to grow our presence within the local community by engaging more with customers and providing consistent dining experiences. Our restaurant became more recognized as a trusted place for affordable and quality meals. Customer loyalty increased significantly, supported by positive feedback, repeat visits, and community support.

Year

2025

Digital Growth and Service Enhancement

We strengthened our online presence by improving our digital marketing strategies and expanding customer engagement through social media platforms. Building Customer Trust
