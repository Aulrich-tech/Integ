Welcome to Your Home Away From Home

Stepping into House of Us feels less like entering a restaurant and more like walking into a friend's dining room. Established in 2013, we are a family-style sanctuary in Valenzuela dedicated to the art of freshly cooked comfort food. Whether you're looking for a quiet corner for a solo study session or a long table for a lively family gathering, our cozy atmosphere is designed to make you feel right at home.
