THE FOUNDER

THE HEART OF THE HOUSE

House of Us began in 2019 with a simple vision by Karen Rose Ocampo: to create a space where the Caloocan community could enjoy the same warmth and flavors found in a family kitchen. With a passion for comfort food and a belief that every guest should be treated like family, Karen Rose Ocampo turned a love for home-cooking into a local landmark.
